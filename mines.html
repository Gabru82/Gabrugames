How to Play

🎯 Objective: Click on safe tiles and avoid the mines!

  • 💰 Enter your bet amount (minimum 10 INR)
  • 💣 Choose number of mines (minimum 3)
  • 🟩 Click tiles to reveal safe spots
  • 🤑 Each safe click increases your profit
  • 🏃‍♂️ Click "Cash Out" to claim your winnings
  • ☠️ If you hit a mine, you lose the bet
  • 🔁 Use Auto Game to auto-play with selected tiles
